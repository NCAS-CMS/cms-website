**Location:** In person: University of Leeds

The course is a mix of practical exercises and informal lectures covering a range of topics including: 
running UM suites, managing Cylc workflows, handling UM data files, and troubleshooting common problems.

For further details, including how to apply, see the [NCAS Training pages](https://www.ncas.ac.uk/en/introduction-to-unified-model-course)
